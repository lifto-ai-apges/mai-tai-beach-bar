The Experience

Palawan Serenity

Mai Tai Port Barton is designed as a peaceful oasis for travelers seeking a slower, more authentic slice of island life. Situated on the quiet shores of Itaytay Beach, this branch embraces sustainable eco-friendly practices throughout its operations.

Relax in cozy bamboo cabanas, sink into beachfront bean bags, and listen to the soothing sound of calm ocean waves. It's the perfect environment for slow lunch dates, afternoon reading sessions, or an inspiring beachfront workspace using our high-speed Starlink internet.
