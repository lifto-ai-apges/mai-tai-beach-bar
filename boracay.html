The Experience

Boracay Beach Vibe

Mai Tai Boracay brings high-energy tropical luxury to the world-famous White Beach. Positioned in the heart of Station 2, our branch is the ultimate gathering spot for beach lovers, cocktail connoisseurs, and sunset chasers.

As afternoon turns into evening, feel the transition from laid-back beachside dining to an electric twilight party. Sip on our signature fresh mango drinks, dance to curated house beats from local DJs, and watch our breathtaking nightly fire dance performance right on the sand.

Got Questions?

Boracay Branch FAQs

Everything you need to know about directions, reservation policies, and entertainment at our Boracay location.

We are located right on the beachfront pathway of Station 2, Barangay Balabag. If you're walking along the beach, we are situated between D'Mall and Station 3. Any tricycle driver can take you to the Station 2 beachfront access path near Hennan Regent, and we are just a short 2-minute walk from the main road.
Our sunset happy hour begins at 4:00 PM daily. The famous fire dance shows and DJ sessions start at 5:30 PM just as the sun sets over the horizon.
Walk-ins are welcome, but beachfront tables are highly popular during sunset peak hours (4:30 PM - 6:30 PM). We strongly recommend reserving a table online in advance to guarantee the best views.
