The Experience

Mai Tai Beach Bar is a true melting pot of Boracay life, welcoming everyone from all backgrounds. Tourists, expats, locals, backpackers, high-end travelers, all ages, cultures, and lifestyles come together in one place, creating a vibrant, inclusive, and authentic island atmosphere.

Located in a premium beachfront position in Station 1, Mai Tai Beach Bar stands out with its unique three-level layout. Each level offers a distinct experience: the Ground floor is high-energy, festive, and social; the Mid level offers a balanced dining and drinks atmosphere; and the Rooftop provides a relaxed, scenic, and premium sunset experience. This structure allows guests to move freely between party energy and chill vibes, making the venue appealing to all.

Boracay Branch FAQs

Everything you need to know about directions, reservation policies, and entertainment at our Boracay location.

We are located right on the beachfront pathway of Station 1, Barangay Balabag. If you're walking along the beach, we are situated near Station 1's famous rock formations. Any tricycle driver can take you to the Station 1 beachfront access path, and we are just a short walk away.
Our sunset happy hour begins at 4:00 PM daily. The famous fire dance shows and DJ sessions start at 5:30 PM just as the sun sets over the horizon.
Walk-ins are welcome, but beachfront tables are highly popular during sunset peak hours (4:30 PM - 6:30 PM). We don't take online reservations, so we suggest arriving early to grab the best views. For large groups or special events, just message us on Facebook Messenger or Instagram.
