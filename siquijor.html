The Experience

Mystical Cliffside Vibe

Mai Tai Siquijor is situated on a stunning cliff edge in San Juan, overlooking the crystal-clear Visayan Sea. Known historically as the "Isla del Fuego" (Island of Fire), Siquijor has a unique, magical charm that is reflected in every corner of this lounge.

As the sun sets, cozy fire pits are lit on our beachside terrace, and tree branches glow with dozens of hanging wicker lanterns. Unwind on low-seated sofas, sip on charcoal-infused cocktails, and lose yourself in the acoustic melodies of local island musicians.

Siquijor Branch FAQs

Read common details about directions to our cliffside lounge, event schedules, and sunset arrival advice.

We are located in Helkat Lane, Solangon, San Juan, Siquijor, right on the scenic sunset cliff edge. We are a 15-minute drive south from Siquijor Port along the main Circumferential Road. Motorbike rental or local tricycles can easily drop you off directly in our private parking lot.
We host live acoustic twilight sessions from 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM on weekdays, and live unplugged local bands with mystical fire dancers on Friday and Saturday nights starting at 6:00 PM.
The sky starts changing to beautiful hues of pink, orange, and purple around 5:00 PM. We recommend arriving around 4:30 PM to secure the best cliffside sofas next to the fire pits and catch the full transition.
